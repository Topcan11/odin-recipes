Classic Burger

Short Description

A classic burger is a delicious sandwich made with a grilled meat or veggie patty placed inside a sliced bun, layered with fresh lettuce, tomato, onion, cheese, and sauces like ketchup, mayonnaise, or mustard. It's a globally loved fast food, customizable for any taste — from beef and chicken to vegetarian styles.

Ingredients

For Patty (Beef or Chicken):

  • 250g ground beef or chicken
  • 1 small onion (finely chopped)
  • 1 tsp garlic paste
  • 1 tsp black pepper
  • Salt to taste
  • 1 tsp paprika or chili powder (optional)
  • 1 tbsp bread crumbs (for binding)
  • 1 egg (optional, for juiciness)

For Veg Patty:

  • 1 boiled potato (mashed)
  • ½ cup grated carrots
  • ½ cup boiled peas or corn
  • 1 tsp chili flakes
  • Salt and pepper to taste
  • Bread crumbs for binding

For Assembling:

  • 2 burger buns
  • Lettuce leaves
  • Tomato slices
  • Onion rings
  • Cheese slices
  • Pickles (optional)
  • Ketchup, mayonnaise, mustard

Steps

  1. Make the Patty: Combine all patty ingredients and form round, flat patties.
  2. Cook the Patty: Grill or fry the patty for 3–4 minutes on each side until cooked through.
  3. Toast the Buns: Lightly toast the inner sides of the buns on a hot pan or grill.
  4. Assemble the Burger: Spread sauces on buns, then layer lettuce, patty, cheese, tomato, onion, and pickles.
  5. Serve: Cover with the top bun and serve hot with fries or a drink.
