Cheesecake

Description

Cheesecake is a rich and creamy dessert made with a smooth cream cheese filling layered over a crunchy biscuit or graham cracker crust. Often topped with fruits, chocolate, or sauces, it’s a timeless treat perfect for special occasions or a sweet indulgence.

Ingredients

For the Crust:

  • 1½ cups crushed graham crackers or digestive biscuits
  • ¼ cup granulated sugar
  • ½ cup melted butter

For the Filling:

  • 450g (2 cups) cream cheese, softened
  • 1 cup granulated sugar
  • 3 large eggs
  • 1 tsp vanilla extract
  • ½ cup sour cream or heavy cream
  • 1 tbsp all-purpose flour (optional for firmness)

Optional Toppings:

  • Fresh fruits (strawberries, blueberries)
  • Fruit compote or jam
  • Chocolate or caramel sauce

Steps

  1. Preheat Oven: Preheat your oven to 325°F (160°C).
  2. Prepare the Crust: Mix crushed crackers, sugar, and melted butter. Press into a springform pan and bake for 10 minutes. Let cool.
  3. Make the Filling: Beat cream cheese until smooth. Add sugar, then eggs one at a time. Mix in vanilla, sour cream, and flour if using.
  4. Pour & Smooth: Pour the filling over the cooled crust and smooth the top.
  5. Bake: Bake for 45–55 minutes until the edges are set but the center is slightly jiggly.
  6. Cool: Let it cool at room temperature, then chill in the fridge for at least 4 hours (preferably overnight).
  7. Serve: Top with fruits or sauce before serving. Enjoy!
