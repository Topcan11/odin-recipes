Momo recipe


Description

Momo is a popular South Asian dumpling dish, especially loved in Nepal, Tibet, and India's Himalayan regions. These dumplings are typically filled with meat or vegetables, wrapped in a thin dough, and steamed or fried. They are often served with a spicy tomato-based achar (dipping sauce).

Ingredients

For Dough:

  • 2 cups all-purpose flour
  • Water (as needed)
  • A pinch of salt

For Filling (Veg/Non-Veg):

  • 1 cup finely chopped cabbage
  • 1 cup minced chicken or vegetables
  • ½ cup finely chopped onion
  • 2 tsp garlic paste
  • 1 tsp ginger paste
  • Salt, pepper, and soy sauce to taste
  • Optional: green chilies, coriander, momo masala

For Achar (Sauce):

  • 2 tomatoes (boiled)
  • 2-3 dried red chilies (soaked)
  • 1 tbsp sesame seeds (roasted)
  • 2 garlic cloves
  • Salt
  • Lemon juice

Steps

  1. Make Dough: Mix flour, salt, and water to form a smooth dough. Rest for 30 mins.
  2. Prepare Filling: Mix all filling ingredients in a bowl.
  3. Shape Momos: Roll out small dough balls, fill with mixture, and fold into dumplings.
  4. Steam: Steam momos for about 10–12 minutes or until translucent.
  5. Make Achar: Blend all achar ingredients into a smooth sauce.
  6. Serve: Serve hot with achar on the side!
